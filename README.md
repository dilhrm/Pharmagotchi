# Pharmagotchi
___

**Your AI Powered Health/Pharmacist Buddy**

Pharmagotchi is an android app with a AI-powered buddy that helps you track and maintain your health, medications, and communication with your health care providers.

Pharmagotchi:
- Helps guide you through any medication condition and medication
- Reminds you through notifications to take your medications and track your health
- Responds in accordance to your personal health
- Graphs your health over time
- Provides AI-powered feedback and explanations for anything health-related
- Allows you to connect your account to your health care providers through email
- Automatically sends health updates and critical health messages to your health care providers

Technologies:
- Android Studio
- Kotlin/XML
- Openrouter
- Figma
- More
